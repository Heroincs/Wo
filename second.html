2. As much as possible avoid late night travel using public transport.

a. If avoiding is not possible then be sure to travel only on crowded bus

b.Avoid taking road side cabs

c. Avoid using a bus which has no passenger or few passenger

d. Don't accept lifts from new friends.

e.Along with staying in a public place, night safety for solo travelers also includes not accepting a lift from people you meet or sharing a taxi with them.

f.Be rude if necessary.



If you're being bothered by someone who just doesn't get the message that you don't want their attention, be rude and noisy. They usually back away.
