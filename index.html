How womens are safe in India

About 29% of Indian women have faced molestation or sexual advances in public places. Of these, over 75% did not even file a police complaint, according to the findings of a survey conducted by LocalCircles, a community social media platform, released on the occasion of International Women’s Day 2021. .









Steps to make womens secure


1.Be aware of your surroundings.

2. As much as possible avoid late night travel using public transport.

3.While travelling at night don’t keep your phone in your hand

4.Sexual harassment and its forms.

5.Emergency alert app
